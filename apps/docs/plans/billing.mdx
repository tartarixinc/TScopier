# Billing and invoices

Use **Subscription & Billing** in the app to manage plan state and payment history.

![Billing summary](/images/billing-summary.svg)

## What you can do

- View current plan and status
- See next renewal date
- Review billing history
- Download invoice PDFs
- Compare subscription plans inline

## Open billing page

1. Sign in to `app.tscopier.ai`
2. Open user menu
3. Go to **Subscription & Billing** (`/billing`)

## Billing tips

- Check invoice period and amount after every plan change
- Keep payment method up to date before renewal date
- If status is past due, resolve payment first before troubleshooting copier behavior
