# Welcome to TSCopier

TSCopier helps you copy Telegram trading signals to your MT4/MT5 accounts from the cloud.  
You can connect channels, set risk rules, and run the copier without a VPS or EA installation.

## What you need before you start

- A TSCopier account
- A Telegram account with access to your signal channels
- At least one MT4 or MT5 broker account
- An active subscription if you want live copying

## Core workflow

1. Connect Telegram
2. Connect your MT4/MT5 account
3. Add channels and link them to broker accounts
4. Configure lot size, TP/SL behavior, and filters
5. Monitor execution in Copier Logs and Updates

## Product areas

- **Channels**: add Telegram channels and control what gets copied
- **Account Configuration**: set lot size, stops, and instruction behavior
- **Billing**: manage plan and invoices
- **Affiliate Program**: invite users and track commissions
