# Copier not copying

If signals appear in Telegram but no trade executes, use this checklist.

## Quick checks

1. Confirm Telegram is connected
2. Confirm channel is active in **Channels**
3. Confirm channel is linked to the correct broker account
4. Confirm broker account status is active
5. Check plan status in **Subscription & Billing**

## Validate signal flow

- Look at **Updates** to confirm messages are parsed
- Look at **Copier Logs** to confirm execution attempts

If parsing exists but execution does not, re-check broker status and account configuration.

## Common causes

- Telegram session expired
- Channel added but not linked to any account
- Broker credentials changed and account requires reconnect
- Account is disconnected or disabled
- Subscription status prevents live copying

## What to send support

- Timestamp of a missed signal
- Channel name
- Broker account name/login (mask sensitive details)
- Screenshot of Updates and Copier Logs around the event
