# FAQ

## Do I need to install an EA or VPS?

No. TSCopier is cloud-based and runs from your browser setup.

## Which platforms are supported?

Telegram signal channels to MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 broker accounts.

## How fast are copied trades?

Execution is designed for low latency from parsing to broker dispatch.

## How many accounts can I connect?

Depends on plan. Basic supports one account. Advanced includes multiple accounts and can scale further.

## Does TSCopier read my private Telegram chats?

No. It only accesses channels/groups you connect for signal copying.

## Can I test a channel before going live?

Yes, with Backtest. Replay past channel signals against your copy settings.

## Does TSCopier support range trades and management signals?

Yes. Advanced behavior includes range entries, multi-target handling, break-even, and other management flows.
