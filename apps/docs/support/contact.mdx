# Contact support

For account, setup, or copier issues, contact support from the app.

## Primary support channel

- [Help & Support (in app)](https://app.tscopier.ai/contact-support)

## Before you open a ticket

- Describe what happened and when
- Include page name and account/channel context
- Attach screenshots where possible
- Mention whether issue is ongoing or intermittent
