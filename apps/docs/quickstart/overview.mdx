# Get started in 3 steps

This is the fastest path to go live with TSCopier.

## Before you start

- Confirm you can log in to Telegram
- Have your MT4/MT5 broker credentials ready
- Know which signal channels you want to follow

## Step 1: Connect Telegram

Go to **Channels** and connect your Telegram account with phone, code, and (if enabled) 2FA.

## Step 2: Connect your broker account

Go to **Account & Configuration**, then add your MT4/MT5 account and verify it is active.

## Step 3: Add channels and configure copier rules

Add your channels in **Channels**, link them to your broker account, then review:

- lot size
- TP splits
- allowed/ignored instructions

![Channels overview](/images/channels-overview.svg)

## Tips

- Start with one trusted channel and one account
- Keep settings simple until you see stable execution
- Use **Updates** and **Copier Logs** to validate behavior
